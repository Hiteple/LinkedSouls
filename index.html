LinkedSouls

Tattoo & Piercings

What you'll find with us

Design

At LinkedSouls we understand the importance and the meaning of a symbol, a pattern or even a simple drawing. Everyone deserves the right to share a special bond with their tattoo, and that is why you don't have to worry about anything. Just tell us what is in your interest in your first appointment. We'll handle the rest.

Tattoo

Right on the first appointment you'll get to know one of our designers and tattoo artists, and after introductions you'll get to know him or her a little bit to get confidence and break the ice. The quantity of sessions are dictated by the complexity of the tattoo itself, and this is something that will be talked about in the first place.

Piercings

If this is in your interest, we have an interesting variety of piercings and rings for every place in your skin. Even if we don't currently have the one you're seeking for, we can get it for you in 24 hours so you don't have to wait a lot. We know you're excited to get right on to it.

Support

You'll recieve an "aftercare instructions" list on paper right on the second appointment in the case of tattoos (the same day you'll start), and in the case of a piercing it will be on the very first day. Also you'll get a number to get on touch with your very tattoo artist directly in the case you'll need assistance.
